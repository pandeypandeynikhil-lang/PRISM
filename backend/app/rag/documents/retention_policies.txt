PRISM BANK — RETENTION POLICIES & ESCALATION FRAMEWORK
=========================================================

SECTION 1: CHURN RISK TIERS & RESPONSE MANDATES
-------------------------------------------------
CRITICAL (≥75% churn probability):
- Mandatory senior relationship manager call within 24 hours
- Approve fee waiver up to ₹5,000 without manager sign-off
- Eligible for Premium Upgrade offer at no charge for 3 months
- Complimentary financial wellness consultation
- SLA: Contact attempt within 1 business day

HIGH (50–74% churn probability):
- Outreach via preferred channel within 48 hours
- Fee waiver up to ₹2,000 automatically approved
- Eligible for loyalty bonus on FD/RD renewals (+0.25%)
- SLA: Contact attempt within 3 business days

MEDIUM (25–49% churn probability):
- Automated personalized email + in-app notification
- Targeted product cross-sell based on usage patterns
- SLA: Digital touchpoint within 5 business days

LOW (<25% churn probability):
- Quarterly NPS survey + standard engagement comms
- Proactive product education content

SECTION 2: APPROVED RETENTION OFFERS
--------------------------------------
OFFER TYPE A — FEE WAIVERS:
- Annual maintenance fee waiver (up to 2 years for Critical tier)
- Zero-forex markup for 6 months (Premium/Wealth customers)
- Free NEFT/RTGS transactions for 12 months
- Locker annual fee waiver

OFFER TYPE B — RATE ENHANCEMENTS:
- Savings account rate boost: +0.50% above prevailing rate for 6 months
- Fixed Deposit preferential rate: +0.30% for tenure ≥ 1 year
- Home loan interest reduction: -0.10% for loyal customers (tenure ≥ 3 years)
- Personal loan pre-approved at 1% below standard rate

OFFER TYPE C — PRODUCT UPGRADES:
- Current account → Premium Business Current Account (waived premium)
- Savings → Privilege Savings with dedicated RM access
- Classic credit card → Gold/Platinum upgrade with waived first-year fee
- Basic debit → Premium debit with airport lounge access (3 visits/quarter)

OFFER TYPE D — EXPERIENTIAL & LOYALTY:
- Invitation to PRISM Wealth Summit (Wealth customers)
- Dedicated relationship manager assignment
- Priority banking queue bypass at all branches
- Complimentary financial planning session (60 minutes)

SECTION 3: CHANNEL PROTOCOLS
-----------------------------
PHONE OUTREACH:
- Use customer's name, acknowledge tenure, express genuine appreciation
- Lead with value, not desperation
- Script: "We noticed you've been with us for X years. As a valued customer, we'd like to offer..."
- Never mention competitor offers unless customer brings them up
- Escalate to senior RM if customer explicitly mentions switching

EMAIL OUTREACH:
- Subject line must be personalized (include name and specific benefit)
- Single CTA per email
- Mobile-optimized HTML template mandatory
- Unsubscribe link mandatory per TRAI regulations

SMS:
- Under 160 characters
- Include customer name
- Include opt-out instruction
- Do not send between 9PM–8AM

IN-APP NOTIFICATION:
- Display on next login, not interrupt-style
- Dismissable
- Link to dedicated landing page

SECTION 4: COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY
------------------------------------
- All retention offers must comply with RBI Fair Practices Code
- No differential pricing based on religion, caste, or gender
- Offers above ₹10,000 in value require branch manager approval
- All interactions must be logged in CRM within 24 hours
- Data used for churn prediction must be covered under customer consent at onboarding
- DPDP Act 2023 compliance mandatory for all data usage
