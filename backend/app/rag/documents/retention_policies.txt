PRISM BANK — RETENTION POLICIES & ESCALATION FRAMEWORK v2
===========================================================

SECTION 1: SEISMIC ZONE RISK TIERS & RESPONSE MANDATES
---------------------------------------------------------
ZONE 5 — CRITICAL (CRS 0.81–1.00):
- Mandatory senior relationship manager call within 24 hours
- Fee waiver up to Rs 5,000 without manager sign-off
- Eligible for Premium Upgrade at no charge for 3 months
- Complimentary financial wellness consultation
- SLA: Contact attempt within 1 business day
- Recommended Action: RM intervention with personalized offers

ZONE 4 — HIGH (CRS 0.61–0.80):
- Outreach via preferred channel within 48 hours
- Fee waiver up to Rs 2,000 automatically approved
- Eligible for loyalty bonus on FD/RD renewals (+0.25%)
- SLA: Contact attempt within 3 business days
- Recommended Action: Targeted outreach, service improvement

ZONE 3 — MODERATE (CRS 0.41–0.60):
- Automated personalized email + in-app notification
- Targeted product cross-sell based on usage patterns
- SLA: Digital touchpoint within 5 business days
- Recommended Action: Re-engagement campaigns, product suggestions

ZONE 2 — LOW (CRS 0.21–0.40):
- Quarterly NPS survey + standard engagement comms
- Proactive product education content
- Loyalty program enrolment
- Recommended Action: Strengthen engagement, loyalty programs

ZONE 1 — VERY LOW (CRS 0.00–0.20):
- Standard monthly communication
- Product awareness campaigns
- Recommended Action: Maintain satisfaction, passive communication

SECTION 2: CPI-BASED PRIORITIZATION
--------------------------------------
CPI = α·CRS + β·OS + γ·IS
α = 0.5 (Urgency weight)
β = 0.3 (Business Value weight)
γ = 0.2 (Inclusion Priority weight)

High CPI (>0.6): Immediate RM action, premium offers
Medium CPI (0.4–0.6): Automated outreach + targeted offers
Low CPI (<0.4): Standard engagement programs

SECTION 3: INCLUSION SCORE POLICY
------------------------------------
Customers with IS > 70 (economically underserved) receive:
- No minimum balance penalties for 6 months
- Free basic banking services bundle
- Financial literacy program access
- Priority consideration for loan restructuring

SECTION 4: APPROVED RETENTION OFFERS
--------------------------------------
OFFER TYPE A — FEE WAIVERS:
- Annual maintenance fee waiver (up to 2 years for Zone 5)
- Zero-forex markup for 6 months (Premium/Wealth)
- Free NEFT/RTGS transactions for 12 months
- Locker annual fee waiver

OFFER TYPE B — RATE ENHANCEMENTS:
- Savings account rate boost: +0.50% above prevailing rate for 6 months
- Fixed Deposit preferential rate: +0.30% for tenure >= 1 year
- Home loan interest reduction: -0.10% for loyal customers (tenure >= 3 years)
- Personal loan pre-approved at 1% below standard rate

OFFER TYPE C — PRODUCT UPGRADES:
- Current account to Premium Business Current Account (waived premium)
- Savings to Privilege Savings with dedicated RM access
- Classic credit card to Gold/Platinum upgrade with waived first-year fee
- Basic debit to Premium debit with airport lounge access

OFFER TYPE D — EXPERIENTIAL & LOYALTY:
- Invitation to PRISM Wealth Summit (Wealth customers)
- Dedicated relationship manager assignment
- Priority banking queue bypass at all branches
- Complimentary financial planning session (60 minutes)

SECTION 5: CHANNEL SELECTION RULES
-------------------------------------
- Zone 5 + complaints > 2: mandatory phone call from senior RM
- age < 35 AND digital_login_frequency > 5: prefer in_app or email
- age >= 55 OR account_balance > 500000: prefer phone or branch
- nps_score < 4: phone call from senior RM mandatory
- IS > 70 (underserved): prefer branch or phone (digital gap consideration)

SECTION 6: COMPLIANCE
-----------------------
- All offers must comply with RBI Fair Practices Code
- No differential pricing based on religion, caste, or gender
- Offers above Rs 10,000 require branch manager approval
- DPDP Act 2023 compliance mandatory for all data usage
- DNC list must be checked before any outreach
