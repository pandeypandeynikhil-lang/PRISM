PRISM BANK — PRODUCT ELIGIBILITY & CPI-BASED RULES v2
======================================================

SAVINGS ACCOUNTS
-----------------
Privilege Savings:
- Eligibility: AMB >= Rs 50,000 OR salary >= Rs 1,00,000/month
- Retention use: Upgrade + rate enhancement for Zone 4/5 customers
- CPI trigger: CPI > 0.5 AND account_balance >= 40,000

Prime Savings:
- Eligibility: AMB >= Rs 10,000
- CPI trigger: CPI > 0.3 AND num_products == 1

CREDIT CARDS
------------
Titanium Credit Card:
- Eligibility: credit_score >= 700, annual income >= Rs 3,00,000
- Retention benefit: Waive annual fee (Rs 1,000) for tenure >= 2 years
- CPI trigger: Zone 4 or 5, credit_score >= 700

Platinum Credit Card:
- Eligibility: credit_score >= 750, annual income >= Rs 8,00,000
- Retention benefit: 3 lounge accesses, waived joining fee Rs 5,000
- CPI trigger: Zone 4/5, 3+ products

FIXED DEPOSITS
--------------
- Retention enhancement: +0.30% for renewals
- Pre-mature penalty waived for Zone 5 customers (one-time)
- Special rates: 444 days (8.10%), 555 days (8.25%)

LOANS
-----
Home Loan:
- Rate reset discount: -0.10% for loyal customers (tenure >= 3 years, Zone 4/5)
- Part-prepayment penalty waived once for loyal customers

Personal Loan:
- Pre-approved: credit_score >= 720, salary account with PRISM
- Retention: -1% rate for top-up loans for existing Zone 4/5 customers

INCLUSION-AWARE RULES
----------------------
Rule I1: IF is_score > 70 AND zone >= 3 THEN offer = "Zero Balance + Free Services Bundle"
Rule I2: IF is_score > 70 AND complaints >= 1 THEN offer = "Service Recovery + Financial Literacy"
Rule I3: IF is_score > 60 AND account_balance < 10000 THEN offer = "No Penalty + Free Transactions"

CPI-BASED AUTOMATED RULES
---------------------------
Rule C1: IF cpi > 0.7 AND account_balance >= 100000 THEN offer = "Privilege Savings + Rate Enhancement"
Rule C2: IF cpi > 0.7 AND num_products == 1 AND credit_score >= 700 THEN offer = "Titanium Credit Card Free"
Rule C3: IF cpi > 0.5 AND complaints >= 2 THEN offer = "Senior RM Assignment + Fee Waiver"
Rule C4: IF cpi > 0.5 AND is_active_member == False THEN offer = "Re-engagement + Cashback"
Rule C5: IF cpi > 0.4 AND nps_score <= 5 THEN offer = "Service Recovery + Product Education"
Rule C6: IF cpi > 0.4 AND digital_login_frequency < 2 THEN offer = "Digital Onboarding + App Walkthrough"
Rule C7: IF tenure_months >= 60 AND cpi > 0.3 THEN offer = "Loyalty Milestone Award + FD Offer"
Rule C8: IF estimated_salary >= 2000000 AND cpi > 0.5 THEN offer = "Wealth Management Consultation"

CHANNEL SELECTION (CPI-AWARE)
-------------------------------
- zone_number == 5: phone call from senior RM (mandatory)
- zone_number == 4 AND complaints > 1: phone call mandatory
- zone_number == 4 AND complaints == 0: email or in_app preferred
- zone_number == 3: email or in_app
- zone_number <= 2: in_app or email (low touch)
- is_score > 70: prefer phone or branch (digital gap)
- age >= 55: prefer phone or branch

PROHIBITED ACTIONS
-------------------
- Never offer competing bank products
- Do not offer to customers flagged for fraud investigation
- Do not contact DNC-listed customers
- Do not make credit offers to customers with pending EMI defaults
- Offers > Rs 10,000 in value require branch manager approval
